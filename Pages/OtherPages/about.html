Secondary

2022: I start my GCSEs (Year 9)

My school started GCSEs in year 9. My school made everyone do a langauge, and history or Geography which left me with two options to freely pick from. I was certain on doing History and Geography which left with one choice, seeing as I had my interest in electronic circuits I choose to do Computer Science as my other option. Once the Computer Science course has started I was a fair bit behind the rest of the class but I did replit's course on an introduction to programming with Python, this helped me to catch up with everyone else, by Christmas I was one of the best in the class. I did not end up completeing the replit course but in lessons we were doing Python work anyway so once I had caught up, I was able to do the challenges set by my teachers well.

2022 to 2023: I do my GCSEs (Year 9 to year 10)

We carried on to do Python work all the way through year 10.

2024 Final year of GCSEs (Year 10 and 11)

Towards the end of year 10 and the start of year 11, is when we started the theory side of Computer Science. We still did regular coding alongside the theory.
